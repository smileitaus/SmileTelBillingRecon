KEY FINDINGS:

   - The bug is likely in the ADDRESS matching: Yiros has address "Shop T47 G, BG 1 DFO Jindalee, 16 Amazons Place, Jindalee QLD 4074"

   - Cannot match by phone prefix (mobile numbers are random)

IMPROVED MATCHING STRATEGY:
- Account-based: If unmatched service shares supplier account with matched services, suggest those customers
- Full phone match: Look for exact phone number matches (not just prefix)
- Address: Skip generic prefixes (Shop, Unit, Level, Suite, Lot) and match on street name
- Plan-based: Services with same plan on same account likely belong to same customer
- For account 586992900: Flag as "bulk mobile fleet - may belong to single customer"
